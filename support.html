FAQ

Common questions

How long does shipping take?+
Orders ship within 48 hours and arrive in 2–5 business days depending on your location.
Can I return an opened product?+
Yes. As long as it's within 30 days of delivery, opened or unopened doesn't matter — you get a full refund.
Do earbuds and headphones include a warranty on the battery?+
Battery health is covered for the first year. After that, battery-only replacements are available at a reduced rate.
What if my smartwatch screen is damaged?+
Accidental screen damage isn't covered by the standard warranty, but our repair team can quote a fix within 24 hours.
How do I track my order?+
Head to the Order page and enter your order number and email to see live shipping status.
